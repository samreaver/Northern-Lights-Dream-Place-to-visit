Northern Lights - A Magical Experience

The Northern Lights, or Aurora Borealis, are a breathtaking natural light display seen in high-latitude regions near the Arctic. Countries like Norway, Iceland, and Canada offer some of the best views. The lights appear in mesmerizing colors of green, purple, and blue, making them a dream sight for travelers.
